Balerion

Balerion, called the Black Terror, was a dragon of House Targaryen. He was ridden by King Aegon I Targaryen during the War of Conquest.

Syrax

Syrax was a female dragon. She was ridden solely by Rhaenyra Targaryen. Its name came from a goddess of Valyria

Arrax

Arrax was a dragon ridden by Prince Lucerys Velaryon during the Dance of the Dragons.

Caraxes

Caraxes, also called the Blood Wyrm and Blood Worm, was the dragon ridden by Prince Aemon Targaryen during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen and later by Prince Daemon Targaryen.

Seasmoke

Seasmoke was a light gray dragon. It was big enough for combat during the dance, but still a young dragon, and more agile in the air than his older brothers.

Vermax

Vermax was the dragon ridden by Prince Jacaerys Velaryon. It prospered and grew every year. The dragon became moody when near snow, ice and cold. Vermax's color is not described in the books.

Vhagar

Vhagar was a female dragon ridden by Visenya during the Conquest, alongside Aegon's Balerion the Conqueror and his sister Rhaenys' Meraxes.
